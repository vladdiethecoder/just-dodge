# Prototype Plan: Paper YOMI Triangle

**Question:** Is the 3-action simultaneous-reveal triangle fun and readable?
**Duration:** 1 hour.
**Method:** Index cards, 2 players, 3 actions (Strike / Block / Grab), first to 3 wins.
**Success Criterion:** Both players report at least one "I read you" moment and want a rematch.

## Materials

- 6 index cards or small slips of paper.
- Pen.
- One human opponent (or play both sides honestly).
- Timer (optional).

## Rules

1. Both players select one action and place the card face-down.
2. Count aloud: "One, two, three, reveal."
3. Resolve according to the triangle:
   - Strike beats Grab
   - Block beats Strike
   - Grab beats Block
   - Same action = reset, no point
4. Winner scores one point.
5. First to 3 points wins the duel.

## Playtest Protocol

1. Play at least 10 full duels.
2. After each duel, record:
   - winner,
   - final score,
   - one-line feeling (tense / boring / confusing / fun),
   - any "read" moment where a player predicted the opponent.
3. Switch who starts or vary pacing if you notice patterns.
