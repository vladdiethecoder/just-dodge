# Combat / Grappling Motion-Capture Dataset Survey — 2026-07-17

## Decision summary

**Use now (legally compatible with an offline motion-teacher corpus):**

1. **Harmony4D** — the strongest public source for *two-person* grappling/wrestling/MMA/karate/fencing interaction. Its Hugging Face dataset is MIT and publicly downloadable; it has SMPL meshes and 2D/3D poses rather than authored BVH clips.
2. **CMU Motion Capture Database (direct, not the AMASS repackaging)** — permissive enough for commercial products (except reselling the raw data), public, and has boxing, punch/strike, swordplay, and a two-subject arm-wrestling interaction. It is not true wrestling/judo.
3. **Kyokushin Karate (37 athletes)** — actual Vicon dual-subject opponent captures, CC0, 9.19 GB. Excellent striking/spacing/contact-adjacent teacher; no clinch, throws, locks, or ground fighting.
4. **KungfuAthleteBot** — Apache-2.0, 7.29 GB, 992 video-reconstructed/retargeted Wushu samples; strong sword/staff/fist dynamics but **not optical source mocap and no partner grappling**.

**Useful only if the project stays non-commercial/research-only:** Bandai Namco Motiondataset (fighting BVH; CC BY-NC 4.0), HDM05 (research-purpose terms), and RTK BJJ Kimura trial (CC BY-NC-SA; a real two-person joint lock but only 20 seconds at downsampled 15 Hz).

**Do not use BONES-SEED as a generative motion-model teacher without an explicit commercial license.** Its custom license prohibits a generative model that produces motion data as a commercially substitutable dataset. It has 20 martial-arts clips, but the public card does not substantiate grappling/clinch content.

## AMASS provenance recommendation

Do **not** treat “AMASS” as one homogeneous permissive corpus. Build source-aware partitions:

- `cmu_direct_permissive/`: download direct CMU AMC/C3D and convert under its explicit broad reuse terms.
- `amass_research_only/`: retain only if the experiment stays within AMASS non-commercial scientific-research terms; preserve source dataset field and AMASS license receipt.
- `exclude_or_legal_review/`: any source with source-specific terms that do not permit the intended future commercial model.

For AMASS combat filtering, select source labels **CMU**, **HDM05**, and **KIT** first; run an action-label/preview audit before conversion. Do not claim grappling coverage from those sources.

## Practical ingestion order

1. **Harmony4D**: create a two-person SMPL/contact loader; retain subject IDs, camera IDs, and original sequence provenance. Build paired teacher trajectories rather than flattening the actors independently.
2. **CMU direct**: ingest the explicit boxing/punch/sword/arm-wrestle IDs above into the existing ASF/AMC/BVH pipeline; maintain pair synchronization for 22/23.
3. **Kyokushin**: ingest C3D marker trajectories and construct two-person attacker/defender sequences for the opponent condition; preserve `air/shield/opponent` labels.
4. **KungfuAthleteBot**: use `.pt` SMPL-H as a high-dynamic strike/weapon augmentation only; quarantine its reconstructed-from-video provenance separately from optical mocap.
5. Add HDM05 / Bandai / RTK trial only to a **research-only** experiment branch, never the commercial candidate corpus.

## License caveat

“Offline teacher only” avoids runtime redistribution, but it **does not override training restrictions**. In particular, BONES-SEED’s generative-motion restriction and RTK’s non-commercial AI restriction apply to model training itself. Keep raw files, license text/version, source URL, download date, and a dataset-level allow/deny decision alongside every derived training shard.
