Simulation Method: Brittle FEM + Organic surface deformation
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ELASTIC ZONE       → Force < 130 MPa — flexes slightly (organic quality)
PLASTIC ZONE       → 130–170 MPa — crack propagation, hairline fractures
FRACTURE           → > 170 MPa — splintered fracture (irregular, jagged)
                     Splinters become wound-extenders (cuts on exit)
FUSED PIECES       → Cannot be removed — fracture stays on body
                     Broken bone-plate fragments shift → new injury node
                     Warden unique: destroyed pieces become weapons
                     (bone shards embedded in attacker on close range hit)
