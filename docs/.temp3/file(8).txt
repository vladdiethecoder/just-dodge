RECOMMENDED STACK FOR YOUR CUSTOM ENGINE:
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Cloth/Leather    → Position-Based Dynamics (XPBD)
                   Open source: "PositionBasedDynamics" (Bender et al.)

Chainmail        → Custom rigid body constraint graph
                   Per-ring: mass + 4 angular constraints
                   GPU-parallelized constraint solver

Plate (FEM)      → Corotational tetrahedral FEM
                   Reference: ARCSim (Berkeley) for sheet deformation
                   GPU: JAX-FEM or Acc-FEM for real-time throughput

Rune-Marble      → Voronoi pre-fracture + runtime crack propagation
                   Reference: "Real-Time Deformation and Fracture"
                              Parker & O'Brien (adapted)

Bone Plates      → Brittle FEM with splinter physics
                   Irregular Voronoi + surface normal bias

Visual updates   → Dynamic mesh deformation on GPU
                   Dent maps baked per-frame at impact site
                   Shader: integrity → crack overlay → shatter UV
